About

We have a dedicated tradition of more than three generations in this craft. Our efforts are to merge the traditional quality and modern concepts in the most attractive manner in the texture of our clothes. The jerry which we use is absolutely genuine. We have here some specimen of half fine jerry for your comparison – cost-wise, quality-wise. We hail from Peringammala a small village near Kovalam, Thiruvananthapuram district known as the cradle of Kerala handloom. Our endeavour is to provide you an opportunity to use our reputed and exclusive items suit to every individual needs – Set mundu, Kutti-mundu for small kids, super fine Dhotis for men, gorgeous bridal wears, rich collection of unique saris and comfortable
